What counts as valid evidence?
We accept photos (JPG, PNG), videos (MP4), documents (PDF, DOC, DOCX), and screenshots (PNG). Each file must be under 50MB. Submit as much relevant evidence as needed to support your case — more evidence strengthens your position in disputes.
There is no limit on the number of files you can upload. You can submit multiple photos, videos, and documents. We recommend organizing your evidence by category (photos, documents, communications) for clarity during review.
During the dispute resolution process, a Hyyryn mediator will review all evidence from both parties. The other party may see relevant portions of your evidence if it becomes part of the mediation. Frivolous or irrelevant evidence may be excluded. Be thorough but professional in your submissions.
