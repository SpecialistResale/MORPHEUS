Frequently Asked Questions

What is escrow and how does it work? ▼
Escrow is a secure arrangement where funds are held by a neutral third party (Hyyryn) until agreed-upon conditions are met. When a buyer and pro agree on a transaction, the buyer deposits their payment into escrow. Once the pro completes the work and the buyer confirms satisfaction, Hyyryn releases the funds to the pro. This protects both parties.
How long are funds held in escrow? ▼
Funds are held in escrow until the buyer confirms the work is complete and satisfactory. Most transactions are resolved within 2-5 business days. If the buyer doesn't respond within 30 days, funds are automatically released to the pro. For urgent releases, buyers can approve at any time.
What happens if there's a dispute? ▼
If a buyer and pro cannot agree on whether work is complete, either party can open a dispute. A professional mediator is assigned to review all evidence, including photos, messages, and deliverables. The mediator makes a binding decision within 72 hours. This ensures fair resolution for both parties.
How do I become a verified Pro? ▼
Becoming a Pro involves completing our five-step verification process: identity verification, background check, skills assessment, portfolio review, and references. The entire process takes 3-5 business days. Once verified, you can create unlimited listings and start accepting jobs. Learn more about Pro verification.
What fees does Hyyryn charge? ▼
Buyers can use Hyyryn for free. Pros are charged a transaction fee ranging from 4% to 10% depending on job value. There are no monthly fees, no listing fees, and no hidden charges. All fees are deducted from the pro's payment after the buyer confirms completion. View our detailed pricing.
