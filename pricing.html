Common Questions About Pricing

When are fees charged?
Platform fees are charged only when a transaction is completed and the buyer confirms satisfaction. Fees are deducted from the Pro's payment before funds are released. Buyers never pay platform fees on Hyyryn.
Can I negotiate fees for large contracts?
Yes. Enterprise clients with high transaction volumes (£100k+ annually) may qualify for custom pricing and volume discounts. Contact our sales team at enterprise@hyyryn.com to discuss your needs.
Are there any hidden charges or setup fees?
No. Hyyryn has no hidden charges, setup fees, monthly subscriptions, or listing fees. The only costs are the platform transaction fees shown in our fee schedule. What you see is what you pay.
